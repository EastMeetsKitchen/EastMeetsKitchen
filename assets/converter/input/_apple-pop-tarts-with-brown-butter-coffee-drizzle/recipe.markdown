__Ingredients__

(makes 3 pop tarts)

Dough

- 1 cup flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 stick butter, cold
- 1 egg yolk
- 3 tablespoons milk

Filling

- 1 large apple
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- dash salt
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Egg Wash

- 1 Egg
- 2 tablespoons milk

Drizzle

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 1/4 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon instant coffee granules
- 2 tablespoons milk
- pinch of salt
