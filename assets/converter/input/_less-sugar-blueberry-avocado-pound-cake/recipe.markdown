__Ingredients__

 (Makes an 8-inch cake)
 
- 1 1/2 ripe medium avocados
- 1 stick butter
- 3/4 - 1 cups sugar
- 2 eggs and 1 yolk
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon  salt
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon almond extract
- 1/4 cup  blueberries
