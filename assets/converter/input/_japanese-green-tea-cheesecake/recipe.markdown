**Ingredients**

For the green tea cheesecake
- 16 tbsp mascarpone
- 4 tbsp butter
- 6 large eggs, separated
- 3 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 3 tbsp matcha green tea powder, sifted
- 1/4  cup confectioners sugar
- 1/2 cup cake flour
- 3 tbsp cornstarch

For osmanthus cream
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1 tablespoon dried osmanthus flowers
- honey
