__Recipe__

(Makes 8 servings)

- 2 lb pork belly
- 2-3 tablespoons salt
- 3-4 tablespoons oil
- 1/2 teaspoon five spice powder
- 2 star anise
- 1/2 cup fried shallots
- 1/4 cup rice wine
- 1/2 cup soy sauce, divided into 2 parts
- 40 grams rock sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 1/2 cups water
- 2-4 eggs, hard-boiled
