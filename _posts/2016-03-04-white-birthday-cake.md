### White Birthday Cake Recipe

(makes 1 8-inch cake)

- 5 egg whites
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup + 3 tablespoons sugar
- 1/2 cup butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 2 cups cake flour 
- 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
- 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons milk
