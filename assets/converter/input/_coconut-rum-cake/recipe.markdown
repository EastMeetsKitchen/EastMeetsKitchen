**Ingredients**

Cake:

- ¾ stick of butter
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tablespoon vanilla
- 2/3 cup flour
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 1 2/3 cup shredded, sweetened coconut (reserve 1/3 cup for sprinkling on top of cake)
- 4 egg whites

Rum Sauce:

- 1 cup granulated sugar
- ¼  cup water
- 1 stick butter
- ½ cup spiced rum
