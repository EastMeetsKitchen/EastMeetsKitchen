
**Ingredients**

For Meringue:

- 4 egg whites
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 2 tsp cornstarch

For Ginger Raspberry Sauce:
- half pint of rapberries
- 3 tbs sugar
- 1 tbs lemon juice
- 1 tbs fresh ginger juice
- 1/2 tsp powdered ginger

For Whipped Cream:
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 1/2 tbs sugar
- splash vanilla
