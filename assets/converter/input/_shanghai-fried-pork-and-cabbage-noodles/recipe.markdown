**Ingredients**

(3-4 people)

Marinating the Pork:

- 1/2 lb pork, shredded into strips
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp oyster sauce (can use soy sauce)
- 1/2 tsp rice wine 
- 1 tsp corn starch
- dash of pepper
- dash of sugar

Sauce for Noodles:
- 2 tbsp  soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster soy sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp garlic powder

Cabbage and Noodles:

- 1-2 ramen-sized packages of udon noodles
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
